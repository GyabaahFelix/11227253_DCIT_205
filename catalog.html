Our Catalog

Explore our catalog of courses and programs offered by the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana.

Undergraduate Courses

  • DCIT101: Introduction to Computer Science

    An introduction to the fundamental concepts of computer science, including programming, algorithms, and problem-solving.

  • DCIT201: Data Structures and Algorithms

    Exploration of data structures and algorithm design, with a focus on efficiency and problem-solving.

  • DCIT301: Database Management Systems

    Study of database concepts, design, and implementation, including SQL and relational database management.

  • DCIT401: Computer Networks

    An overview of computer networks, protocols, and communication technologies.

  • DCIT501: Software Engineering

    Principles and practices of software development, including software design, testing, and project management.

Graduate Courses

  • DCIT601: Advanced Algorithms

    Advanced topiDCIT in algorithm design and analysis, including dynamic programming, greedy algorithms, and complexity theory.

  • DCIT701: Distributed Systems

    Exploration of distributed computing systems, including distributed algorithms, fault tolerance, and scalability.

  • DCIT801: Artificial Intelligence

    Introduction to artificial intelligence, covering topiDCIT such as machine learning, natural language processing, and expert systems.

  • DCIT901: Machine Learning

    Study of machine learning algorithms, data mining, and applications in various domains.

  • DCIT1001: Big Data Analytics

    Exploration of big data technologies, analytics techniques, and applications in real-world scenarios.
